FAQs

  • How long can the process take?
    The entire process, including document preparation, language proficiency tests, and official procedures like visa issuance and registration, can take approximately 7 to 10 months, depending on individual circumstances and government processing times.
  • How long the job offer contract will be?
    The job offer contract is usually unlimited, providing long-term employment with clear terms on probation, benefits, and notice periods.
  • How long can we stay in germany?
    The initial visa is for 3-6 months and can be extended to 4 years based on employment and compliance with regulations.
  • How long will it take for PR?
    You can apply for permanent residency (PR) after receiving 24 consecutive pay slips, which typically takes around 2 years. This timeframe assumes continuous employment and fulfillment of other residency requirements, such as paying taxes and contributions to social security. Meeting these criteria ensures eligibility for PR, granting you long-term stability and the ability to live and work in Germany without restrictions.
  • How long it take to get German passport?
    To obtain a German passport, it takes 3 years if you reach C1 level in the language, and 5 years if you achieve only B1 level, along with fulfilling other residency requirements.
  • Can I leave my job?
    You can leave your job, but it's not recommended within the first year. If you choose to resign, you must provide a 3-month notice period, as leaving early may impact your residency status and future job prospects.
  • Can i visit back pakistan?
    Yes, you can visit Pakistan, but you'll need to plan your holidays yourself, typically around 30 paid holidays per year. It's important to coordinate with your employer to ensure your absence does not disrupt work.
  • Can i bring my family with me or call them later?
    Yes, you can bring your family with you or call them later to join you. The choice is yours; if you bring them initially, ensure they have the necessary visas. Alternatively, you can apply for family reunification later once you’ve settled in Germany.
  • Can my spouse work?
    Yes, your spouse can work in Germany, which allows for additional household income and better integration. However, their employment will change your tax class, potentially affecting your net income. It's advisable to consult a tax advisor to understand the implications and optimize your tax situation.
